How it works

From stats to lineup in minutes

LineupIQ fits into your existing GameChanger workflow — no manual data entry required.

01

Import your roster

Export your team's batting stats from GameChanger as a CSV. Import it into LineupIQ and your full roster populates instantly with advanced stats computed automatically.

02

Scout tonight's opponent

Upload the opponent's GameChanger box score PDF. LineupIQ reads their pitching stats and auto-tags tendencies like "Walks batters" or "High K rate" that adjust your simulation.

03

Optimize your lineup

Tap Optimize to see three data-driven lineup options. Lock in any player to their slot, apply the order you want, and drag to fine-tune.

04

Simulate game day

Run 2,000 simulated games against tonight's opponent. See your projected run total, confidence range, and which players are driving the most value.
